Ethical Crawling & System Architecture

1. Robots.txt Checker

The crawling engine parses /robots.txt immediately using a custom pre-fetch cache parser. Paths prohibited for crawler User-Agents are strictly skipped, preventing legal and policy violations.

2. Honeypot Detector

Websites place hidden links (e.g., display: none; styles) to detect bots. The parser scans and skips links with hidden css inline attributes, shielding the scraper from trigger bans.

3. Exponential Backoff & Jitter

If rate limits are triggered (HTTP 429) or connection issues occur, the scraper does not flood the server. It waits, increasing the retry sleep time exponentially and injecting a random delay (+/- 20%) to mimic natural user browse cycles.
